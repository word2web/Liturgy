# THE CONFERRING OF MINOR ORDERS 


The degrees from Cleric to Acolyte inclusive may be conferred apart from the celebration of the Holy Eucharist; but only in the morning, except in the case of 
Cleric, which may be given at any hour. If, however,  they are given during the Eucharist, the ordination to the degree of Cleric shall take place after the Introit, and to the other four degrees after the Kyrie.
