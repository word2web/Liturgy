RULES TO KNOW WHEN MOVABLE FEASTS OCCUR. 


Advent Sunday is always the nearest Sunday to the 
Beast of St. Andrew, whether before or after. 


Septuagesima is nine weeks before Easter. 
Sexagesima is eight weeks before Easter. 
Quinquagesima is seven weeks before Easter. 


The First Day of Lent or Ash Wednesday is the 
Wednesday preceding the first Sunday in Lent. 


The First Sunday in Lent or Quadragesima is six 
weeks before Easter. 


Refreshment Sunday is the fourth Sunday in Lent 
or three weeks before Easter. 


Passion Sunday is the fifth Sunday in Lent or two 
weeks before Easter. 


Palm Sunday is one week before Easter. 

Maundy Thursday is the Thursday preceding Easter. 
Good Friday is the Friday preceding Easter. 

Holy Saturday is the Saturday preceding Haster. 


Easter Sunday is always the first Sunday after the 
Full Moon, which happens upon or next after the 
wenty-first day of March; if the Full Moon happen 
pon a Sunday, Easter Day is the Sunday after, 


Ascension Day is forty days after Easter. 

Whitsunday is.seven weeks after Easter. 

Trinity Sunday is eight weeks after Haster. 

Corpus Christi is the Thursday next after Trinity 
Sunday.
